Dr. Carlos Enrique Améndola Cerón
Email: carlos.amendola (at) tum.de

I am a postdoc at TU Munich in the research groups Mathematical Statistics led by Claudia Klüppelberg and Optimization and Data Analysis led by Felix Krahmer.

My main research interest is Algebraic Statistics. I am also very interested in developments concerning Applied Algebraic Geometry.

I graduated as a Mathematics PhD student in Bernd Sturmfels' Einstein Fellowship research group in "Nonlinear Algebra" at TU Berlin. My co-advisor was Christian Haase.

I have a B.S. in Applied Mathematics from Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México and an additional B.S. in Mathematics degree from Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México . I also obtained an M.S. in Mathematics from Courant Institute at New York University.
